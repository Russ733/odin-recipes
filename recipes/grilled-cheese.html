The Best Grilled Cheese on Earth

Description

Even something as simple and easy as a Grilled Cheese should be delicious. Common household ingredients come together for a perfect taste on a timeless classic.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of white bread
  • Butter
  • 3 slices of white American cheese
  • Salt and Pepper

Directions

  1. Spread butter evenly on one side of each slice of bread, making sure to cover entire surface
  2. Add salt and pepper to buttered side of both slices of bread
  3. Heat a pan over medium heat and add one slice of bread, buttered side down
  4. Add 3 slices of American cheese to bread in pan, top with remaining slice of bread with the buttered side up
  5. After 3-4 minutes, flip sandwich over in pan and cook for an additional 3-4 minutes. Continue this process until desired toastiness is reach on both sides
  6. Remove Grilled Cheese from pan, slice diagonally with one half larger than the other
  7. Enjoy!
