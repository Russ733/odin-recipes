Ricotta Pizza

Description

A quick, simple and tasty homemade pizza! A creamy ricotta base, topped with fresh zucchini and tomatoes, finished with a drizzle of honey and a sprinkle of red pepper flakes.

Ingredients

  • Pizza Crust
  • 1 large Zucchini
  • 10 oz Cherry Tomatoes
  • 1/2 pint Ricotta Cheese
  • 1 tbsp Italian Seasoning
  • 1 tbsp Garlic Powder
  • Honey
  • Salt & Pepper (to taste)

Directions

  1. Pre-heat oven to 400 degrees
  2. Thinly slice zucchini and quarter cherry tomatoes
