Ricotta Pizza

Description

A quick, simple and tasty homemade pizza! A creamy ricotta base, topped with fresh zucchini and tomatoes, finished with a drizzle of honey and a sprinkle of red pepper flakes.

Ingredients

  • Pizza Crust
  • 1 large Zucchini
  • 10 oz Cherry Tomatoes
  • 1/2 pint Ricotta Cheese
  • 1 tbsp Italian Seasoning
  • 1 tbsp Garlic Powder
  • Honey
  • Olive oil
  • Salt & Pepper (to taste)

Directions

  1. Pre-heat oven to 400 degrees
  2. Thinly slice zucchini and quarter cherry tomatoes
  3. Heat a small drizzle of olive oil in a pan
  4. Cook zucchini in pan until tender, remove from pan
  5. Heat another small drizzle of olive oil in the same pan
  6. Add tomatoes, half of the Italian seasoning, half of the garlic powder, salt and pepper
  7. Cook tomatoes in pan for 3-4 minutes
  8. In a small mixing bowl, mix together ricotta cheese, remaining Italian seasoning, remaining garlic powder, olive oil, salt and pepper
  9. Spread a thin layer of ricotta mix onto pizza crust
  10. Add zucchini slices and top with tomatoes
  11. Bake for 12-15 minutes
  12. After baking, remove from oven and allow to cool for several minutes
  13. Drizzle honey over pizza
  14. Slice and enjoy!

Enjoy topped with parmesan cheese and red pepper flakes for added flavor!
