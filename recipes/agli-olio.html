Agli Olio

Description

Classic Italian pasta dish with garlic, onions and olive oil.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb Linguine
  • 1 Large Sweet Onion
  • 5 Cloves of Garlic
  • Olive Oil
  • 1 tbsp Italian Seasoning
  • Parsley

Directions

  1. Bring 8 quarts of water to a boil in a large pot
  2. Thinly slice onion and mince garlic cloves
  3. Heat a drizzle of olive oil in a large pan or skillet and add onions. Cook 5-6 minutes or until soft
  4. Add linguine to boiling water and cook until it has reached desired tenderness
  5. Once onions have softened, add another drizzle of olive oil to pan with onions, add garlic and Italian seaoning
  6. Drain linguine, reserving 1/2 cup pasta water
  7. Add pasta water to pan, a small splash at a time, and mix into onions and garlic until they are covered
  8. Simmer onions about 5 minutes, or until liquid has started to thicken. Turn off heat
  9. Add linguine to pan and mix thoroughly with onions and garlic
  10. Drizzle pasta mixture with olive oil, garnish with parsley
  11. Enjoy!
